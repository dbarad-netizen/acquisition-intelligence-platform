Data: real, active business-license records from the City of Los Angeles Office of Finance open-data portal (NAICS 238220, 238210, 238160; pulled July 2026). Only license fields are real; every other field is unknown until enriched, which is why records sit at Low confidence and mostly Watch tier — enrichment (CSLB license age, Google Places, LLM website analysis, Data Axle) is what promotes them to Acquire or Broker. Note: city license start dates reset on re-registration, so true business age needs CSLB license issue dates. Scores follow the v2 specification in the strategic document: Business Quality and Transition Probability (per the original plan, rebalanced), plus two new scores — Brokerage Fit and AI Modernization Upside. Data Confidence reflects verified vs. estimated field coverage. In production these fields populate from CSLB/licensing boards, Data Axle, Google Places, assessor records, LLM website analysis, and logged owner conversations.
